About Me

I am currently working as a Principal Research Scientist at Nokia Bell Labs in Cambridge, UK and lead the Device Systems team. My research interests include mobile systems, mobile sensing, Internet of Things (IoT), social and culture computing. I enjoy building real, working systems and applications and like to collobrate with other domain experts for interdisciplinary research.
